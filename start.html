Welcome to Conquest of the Mongols, a choose-your-adventure game designed by Sean Snider for a class at Duke University called "Between Moscow, Beijing, and Delhi", taught by Dr. Mustafa Tuna.
In this game, you will be faced with a series of decisions, and what you pick has consequences. Your goal is simple - if you are playing as the Mongols, conquer a civilization. Otherwise, avoid getting conquered.


First, choose your level.

     
  1. Easy - You will play as Shah Ala ad-Din Muhammad of the Khwarazmian Empire. You have heard rumors of a nomadic society growing extremely powerful in the steppes to your west. Can you avoid getting wiped from the history books by a Mongol invasion?
    2.  
  2. Medium - You are Genghis Khan himself, and your goal is to conquer the Jin Dynasty in modern-day China. Genghis Khan was not able to finish the job in his lifetime - can you rewrite history?
    3.  
  3. Hard - guide Kublai Khan and his armies in their conquest of the Song Dynasty, often described as the most difficult conquest in Mongol histroy. Can you unite China under the Yuan dyansty, as Great Kublai did so long ago?
