Welcome, Great Temujin!

You were the original Great Khan of the Mongols, and formed the basis for what would soon become the largest contiguous empire in history shortly after your death. But in the early 13th Century, your lands weren't nearly what they would be someday, and the status quo hadn't yet been truly upended. That would quickly change, though, when a delegation from the Jin Dynasty arrived at your court to proclaim the new emperor.
